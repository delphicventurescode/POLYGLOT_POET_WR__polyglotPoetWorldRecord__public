**The World Record**

Polyglot_poet: Most poetry books in two consecutive years across natural and constructed languages.

---
**Requirements**

- the years have to be consecutive.
- at least one poetry book in a constructed language and at least one poetry book in a natural language
  
---
**Discussion**

Over two consecutive years, I authored the most poetry books spanning natural and constructed languages. This record demonstrates linguistic creativity and poetic expression across both established natural languages and constructed languages. The collection showcases the intersection of poetry, linguistics, and constructed language communities, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in contemporary poetry publishing.

This accomplishment required not only prolific writing and publishing capability, but also deep engagement with linguistic innovation. Each book represents a distinct voice and perspective, whether written in widely-spoken languages or in constructed languages. The diversity of linguistic mediums reflects a commitment to exploring how language itself can be a tool for poetic expression.

This record stands as a testament to the power of multilingual creativity and the growing recognition of constructed languages as legitimate mediums for literary art. By bridging traditional poetry communities with constructed language enthusiasts, this achievement opens new conversations about the nature of language, translation, and artistic expression in our increasingly connected world.
